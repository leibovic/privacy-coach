Firefox Privacy Coach

by Mozilla

What it does

  • Preference Tweaks
    • Tracking = Do Not Track
    • Cookies = Enabled, excluding third-party
    • Crash reporter = disabled by default
    • Telemetry = disabled by default
    • Firefox Health Report = disabled by default
    • MozStumbler = disabled by default

    Fir
  • Look stealth with 13fox
    A lightweight theme that's dark.
  • HTTP Warning
    Instant notification if your search is going over HTTP, prohibits you from adding HTTP search engines.

More Information

Even though Privacy Coach takes care of a lot of privacy-related preferences, here are a few more Add-ons that can make your browsing experience even more awesome:

  • Duck Duck Go (HTTPS/SSL) – DuckDuckGo is the search engine that doesn't track you. This extension adds DuckDuckGo (HTTPS / SSL version) to the search bar.
  • Ghostery – Protect your privacy. See who's tracking your web browsing and block them.
  • HTTPS Everywhere – encrypts your communications with many major websites, making your browsing more secure.
  • Self Destructing Cookies – Gets rid of a site's cookies and LocalStorage as soon as you close its tabs. Protects against trackers and zombie-cookies. Trustworthy services can be whitelisted.
